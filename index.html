About Fuzzy Friends Rabbitry

Fuzzy Friends Rabbitry is a small, home-based rabbitry located in Winchester, VA. We specialize in raising Holland Lop bunnies known for both their quality and gentle temperament. Holland Lops are one of the sweetest breeds and stay relatively small (around 4–5 lbs), making them a great fit for families. I started this rabbitry with the goal of raising well-loved, friendly bunnies and connecting them with homes where they’ll be truly cared for. Every rabbit is handled regularly—including by children—so they are comfortable, social, and used to being part of a family before they go home. Each bunny also gets time outside to hop and explore in our outdoor pens

We believe the way a bunny is raised matters. That is why we focus on calm care, consistency, and creating a positive start for every baby before they head home.
